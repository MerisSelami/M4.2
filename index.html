Welcome

Whether you’re just getting started or already know your way around local trails and wetlands, this is your stop for resources, routes, and community birding tips.

Explore checklists, learn how to identify calls, and find the best gear to bring on your next outing. We share weekly notes and sightings from around the area so you can plan the perfect time to get outside.

So don’t waste time! Get what you need, then turn off that computer and get out into the great outdoors!
